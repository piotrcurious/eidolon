Paradigm: Top-Down Blackbox Waveform Synthesis. Higher musical layer form (progression seeds, phrase density, and dynamic text-phoneme inputs per bar) strictly guides seeded probabilistic Markov chains. These chains generate an implicit spectral colormap which is immediately reconstructed into the actual final waveform via additive resynthesis. Formant Modulation: The text inputs in the 'Guided Formant' panel parse phonemes to dynamically modulate formants per bar. Users have loose parameters via sliders influencing the stochastic generation but cannot view nor edit intermediate stages of colormap or Volterra reconstruction. Tuning construction leverages seeded Fifth-chain MOS scale generalisation. Volterra Series memory-polynomials provide complex harmonic texture derived implicitly from form. Tap any generated chord cell in the Progression Strip to swap its function or lock it against future random regeneration.
