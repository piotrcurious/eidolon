Signal path: a seeded Markov chain drives chord function → a second Markov chain (with leap-recovery and rhythmic continuity) generates the lead phrase over that progression → each sounding note gets a harmonic template from a truncated Volterra memory-polynomial (orders 1–4, closed-form via the cosine multiple-angle / Chebyshev identities) shaped by a formant resonance envelope → a per-harmonic discrete-state Markov chain (mean-reverting over quantized amplitude levels) evolves that template into the spectral image shown above → additive resynthesis with continuous phase accumulation renders the waveform. Everything is deterministic for a given seed.
