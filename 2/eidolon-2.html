Signal path: a seeded Markov chain drives chord function (now over a jazz-capable vocabulary with secondary dominants when Style is set to Jazz, plus tritone substitution and extensions as post-processing) → a second Markov chain generates the lead phrase over that progression → each note's harmonic template comes from a truncated Volterra memory-polynomial shaped by a formant envelope → a per-harmonic Markov chain (five selectable distribution families) evolves that into the spectral image shown above → additive resynthesis renders the waveform. Tuning generalizes the whole harmonic layer to any EDO via MOS (fifth-chain) scale construction and just-intonation-ratio chord qualities mapped to the nearest step — at 12-EDO this reproduces standard equal temperament exactly. Tap any chord in the progression strip to swap its function or lock it against future regeneration; edits re-render the phrase and audio immediately without disturbing the seed.
